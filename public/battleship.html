⚓ How to Play Battleship

🚢
Place your ships

Click and drag across your grid to place each ship. The direction of your drag sets horizontal or vertical. Right-click anywhere on the grid to rotate.

🎯
Take turns firing

When it's your turn, click any cell on the enemy grid to fire a shot. Grey = miss, Red = hit.

💥
Hits give you another turn!

Land a hit and you get to fire again immediately. Miss and it's your opponent's turn.

🏆
Sink all ships to win

First player to sink all 5 enemy ships — Carrier, Battleship, Cruiser, Submarine, and Destroyer — wins!
